Maggie

Ingredients:

  • Noodle
  • TasteMaker

Recipe:

  1. Sauté the aromatics: Heat oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and fry for 1 minute until soft.
  2. Cook the vegies : Toss in your mixed vegetables and tomatoes. Sauté for 2 minutes until they soften slightly but remain crunchy.
  3. Cook the noodle: Break the Maggi noodle cake into the pan. Empty the Maggi Tastemaker packet over the top and stir thoroughly.
